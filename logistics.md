## Venue
The workshop takes place at the guest house of [Goethe University](https://www.goethe-university-frankfurt.de/), next to **[Campus Westend](https://www.goethe-university-frankfurt.de/44702769/Campus_Westend)** and the **[Botanical Garden](https://www.botanischergarten-frankfurt.de/)**.

- **Address:** [Frauenlobstraße 1, 60487 Frankfurt am Main](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BvFwTYJuvQVHOnM1I)  
- **Guest house info:** [Goethe University guest houses](https://www.goethe-university-frankfurt.de/45731032/Guesthouses-Ditmarstrasse-and-Frauenlobstrasse)

## Getting there

### From the airport
- **S-Bahn + U-Bahn (~30 min, ~€5):** Take the [S8 or S9](https://www.rmv.de/c/en/) toward the city centre to *Hauptwache*. Transfer to the U6 (direction Praunheim) or U7 (direction Hausen) and exit at *[Bockenheimer Warte](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YmGHv7XkKqQZbKDd9)*. From there it is a 3-minute walk to the venue.
- **Taxi / ride (~15–20 min, ~€40–50)**

### From the main station (Hauptbahnhof)
- **U-Bahn (~15 min):** Take the U4 (direction Bockenheimer Warte) to the end station *[Bockenheimer Warte](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YmGHv7XkKqQZbKDd9)*. From there it is a 3-minute walk north along Miquelallee, then right onto Frauenlobstraße.

### From Motel One Römer / city centre
- **U-Bahn (~15 min):** From *Dom/Römer*, take the U4 (direction Bockenheimer Warte) to the end station *Bockenheimer Warte*, then walk 3 minutes.
- **Walking (~35 min):** Head west along Berliner Straße / Bockenheimer Landstraße toward Bockenheimer Warte.

> **Tip:** A single ticket within Frankfurt (Preisstufe 1) costs around €3. You can buy tickets at RMV machines in any station or via the [RMV app](https://www.rmv.de/c/en/). All tickets are valid across S-Bahn, U-Bahn, tram, and bus.
